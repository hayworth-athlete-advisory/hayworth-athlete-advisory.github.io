About

Zachary Hayworth, a certified agent with the National Basketball Players' Association, oversees Hayworth Sports Group. After founding his first company as a teenager, Zachary went on to receive multiple academic degrees and advanced training at five different universities. He also operates a business consulting firm, Hayworth & Co., which offers unlimited and unparalleled services and resources to all clients of H.S.G..

Zachary received his Bachelor of Science degrees in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey. He later obtained a Master of Business Administration in management from the University of Massachusetts, where he graduated with distinction and highest honors.

In addition, Zachary obtained a master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina. At Duke, he pursued in-depth research and a specialized academic track tailored to sports business, sports law, and emerging managerial issues in sports. At Duke, as well as independently, Zachary has also conducted extensive research and produced numerous reports and papers on athlete representation, financial management for athletes, risks and regulation in sports, and technology in the sports industry.
