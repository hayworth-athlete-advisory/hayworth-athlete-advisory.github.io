About

Zachary Hayworth is a certified agent with the National Basketball Players’ Association. He holds an in-depth knowledge of the NBA-NBPA Collective Bargaining Agreement including contracts, compensation, benefits, options, player rights, trade rules, grievance and arbitration, and other critical elements. Zachary Hayworth has served as a private advisor and consultant for various athletes.

In addition to his extensive professional and academic work in sports, Zachary is a former entrepreneur. He founded an e-commerce company in his early teenager years that eventually evolved into a major wholesaler. More recently, Zachary has served as a consultant for a wide range of individuals and businesses, advising them on matters related to communication, entrepreneurial strategy, and general business management. He current holds minority ownership positions in several other ventures.

Zachary Hayworth received his Bachelor of Science degrees in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey. He later obtained a Master of Business Administration in management from the University of Massachusetts, where he graduated with highest distinction and a 3.97 grade point average.

In addition, Zachary obtained a master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina. He pursued a specialized academic track tailored to sports business, sports law, and emerging managerial issues in sports. At Duke, as well as independently, Zachary has conducted extensive research and produced numerous reports and papers on athlete representation, financial management for athletes, sports regulation, and the impact of technology within sports.
