About

Zachary Hayworth, a certified agent with the National Basketball Players' Association, oversees Hayworth Sports Group and has advised many athletes at the professional level. A lifelong entrepreneur, Zachary started his first company as a teenager and has nearly 15 years of experience with managerial, financial, and strategic matters. In conjunction, Zachary leads a business consulting firm, Hayworth & Co., which provides unparalleled resources and services to clients of Hayworth Sports Group.

Zachary received highly advanced training at five different universities. His academic credentials include a Bachelor of Science in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey, and a Master of Business Administration in management from the University of Massachusetts, where he graduated with distinction and highest honors.

In addition, Zachary obtained an advanced master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina. At Duke, he pursued a specialized academic track and associated research tailored to advanced business strategy, sports business, and sports law. At Duke, as well as independently, Zachary has also produced numerous reports and research on athlete representation, athlete management, financial strategies for athletes, sports technology, and league regulations.
