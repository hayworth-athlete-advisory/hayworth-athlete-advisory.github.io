About

Zachary Hayworth is a certified agent with the National Basketball Players' Association.

He holds an in-depth knowledge of the NBA-NBPA Collective Bargaining Agreement including contracts, compensation, benefits, options, player rights, and other critical elements. Zachary has also served as a private advisor and consultant for athletes on a wide range of business and personal matters.

Zachary received his Bachelor of Science degrees in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey. He later obtained a Master of Business Administration in management from the University of Massachusetts, where he graduated with highest distinction and a 3.97 grade point average. In addition, Zachary is a graduate of Duke University in Durham, North Carolina. At Duke, he obtained a specialized master's degree with an academic track tailored to the business of sports, sports law, and managerial issues in sports. Zachary has conducted extensive research and produced numerous reports and papers focused on athlete representation, financial management for athletes, regulation in sports, and technology’s impact on athlete brands.
