About

Zachary Hayworth, a certified agent with the National Basketball Players' Association, oversees Hayworth Sports Group. A lifelong entrepreneur, Zachary started his first company as a teenager. Prior to founding Hayworth Sports, he gained nearly 15 years of experience overseeing managerial, financial, and strategic matters in an ownership capacity. Zachary also runs a business consulting firm, Hayworth & Co., which provides unparalleled resources and services to clients of Hayworth Sports Group.

Zachary received highly specialized training at five different universities. His academic credentials include a Bachelor of Science in business administration and entrepreneurship from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey, and a Master of Business Administration in management from the University of Massachusetts. He completed all of his degrees with distinction and highest honors.

In addition, Zachary obtained a master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina, which was tailored to sports business, sports law, and advanced management studies. At Duke, as well as independently, Zachary has produced numerous reports and research on athlete representation, financial strategies for athletes, managerial issues in sports, and league regulations.
