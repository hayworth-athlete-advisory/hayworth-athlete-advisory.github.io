About

Zachary Hayworth, a certified agent with the National Basketball Players' Association, formed Hayworth Sports Group after a longtime tenure as an entrepreneur. Zachary became a business owner as a teenager and has over 15 years of experience overseeing managerial, financial, and strategic matters. Following his exit from ownership, he opereated a consulting service that advised a wide range of professionals and organizations. Most recently, he founded Hayworth Sports Group to offer a non-traditional approach to representation, management, and advisory for professional athletes. Using his extensive business experience and training, Zachary uses sophisticated analysis and entrepreneurial principles to create value for his clients in professional sports.

Over the course of his career, Zachary has received highly specialized training at five different universities. His educational background includes undergraduate degrees in business administration and entrepreneurship with honors from Rowan University in Glassboro, New Jersey, and a Master of Business Administration in management from the University of Massachusetts. In addition, Zachary holds a master's degree from Duke University in Durham, North Carolina, where he pursued an academic track tailored to advanced business studies, sports and law, and the business of sports. At Duke, as well as independently, Zachary has conducted in-depth research and produced numerous reports on athlete representation, financial management for athletes, regulation in sports, and emerging issues within the sports industry. He completed each of his degrees with distinction and highest honors.
